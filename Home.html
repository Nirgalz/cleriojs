Take a look at OUR AMAZING PRODUCTS

  • Maki Rolls

    A small segment cut from a long roll of rice and various other ingredients wrapped in a sheet of seaweed.

  • Gunkan

    This style of sushi is called a gunkan-maki battle ship roll, since the tight nori wrap resembles battleships.

  • Sashimi

    Sashimi is a Japanese delicacy consisting of very fresh fish sliced into thin pieces.

  • Nigiri

    Sushi consisting of a small ball of rice with a touch of wasabi and topped with raw fish or other seafood.
